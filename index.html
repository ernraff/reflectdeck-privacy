Privacy Policy for ReflectDeck

Effective Date: 4/10/2025

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. ReflectDeck is designed to run entirely on your device.

2. Local Storage Only

All data you create — including affirmations and audio recordings — is stored locally on your device. We do not access, transmit, or back up this data to any server.

3. Microphone Access

The app may request access to your microphone solely to record affirmations. These recordings remain on your device and are not shared.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not use any third-party services or share your data with others. ReflectDeck is a standalone app with no integrations.

5. Your Control

You can delete any saved data within the app by removing affirmations individually or uninstalling the app.
